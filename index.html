Our Message

A calming way of life

AI is now being used in a wide range of fields. If you ask this AI, "How much of our universe is invisible?" it will answer, "Only 5% of the universe is made up of visible matter, while approximately 95% is invisible."

These invisible things are strongly connected to our daily lives and emotions, such as the happiness and sense of security we feel when we experience compassion and kindness in our daily lives, the moving tears we shed at the sight of a sunrise or sunset, and the pleasant feeling of a gentle breeze in nature.

I founded Aura Orientalis with the desire to be a company that creates products and provides services that value these invisible feelings of happiness, security, sense of wonder and calming.
